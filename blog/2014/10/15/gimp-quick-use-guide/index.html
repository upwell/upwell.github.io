Gimp Quick Use Guide

Oct 15th, 2014

Copy layer to a new image

Use ctrl+c and ctrl+v to copy and paste a layer.

Crop to selection

Use Crop tool to select the area to crop, and then adjust the params in the toolbox windows, after all done, double click the selection.
